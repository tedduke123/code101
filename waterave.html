Coffee on the SE waterfront

Situated on the SE Portland waterfront near OMSI, Water Avenue has been in business since 2009 and is associated with the American Barista School. Water Avenue has a 1974 Samiac roaster which is on full display for patrons to witness coffee roasting while they sip their lattes. A full food menu is offered in addition to an extensive coffee selection. Hop over next door to purvey home coffee equipment at Clive when you are finished.

1028 SE Water Ave #145, Portland, OR 97214
